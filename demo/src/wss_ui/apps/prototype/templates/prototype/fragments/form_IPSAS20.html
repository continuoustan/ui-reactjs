The IPSAS-20 "Related Party Disclosures" standard requires the disclosure of specific financial information on related parties.

The standard defines various entities and individuals as related parties and sets out the disclosures required in respect of those parties, including the compensation of key management personnel.

This information has been disclosed in the WFP Financial Statements since IPSAS adoption at WFP and is now being requested for the 2016 Financial Statements.

Related parties can include key management personnel and close family members.

Key management personnel

Key management personnel are defined under IPSAS as those officials who are responsible for the planning, directing and controlling activities of the entity. WFP includes the Executive director, the Deputy executive director and Assistance executive directors into the key management personnel group and includes all Regional directors and Divisional directors in HQ into the other senior management personnel group.
